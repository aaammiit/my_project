RightAdvise

Every year, Indian students seeking admissions in Universities abroad face a critical, crucial stumbling block. Be it for an undergraduate degree, a master’s programme or for higher, specialized studies, most universities rate and judge applications based on the submissions made. Apart from scholastic records, college admissions rely heavily on “getting to know” a candidature. They strive to get a better understanding of each applicant and look to determine what makes each applicant unique. Equally, they look for clarity in thought and expression, consistency, and above all, how can you contribute beyond academic rigour. RightWrite has developed a MEAL framework that aids students in standing apart from the clutter:

  • Measure: We have a conversation with each applicant to better understand their interests, aspirations, and their primary objective
  • Engage: Each applicant is requested to jot down their thoughts and accomplishments – beyond academics and GPAs – and, also why they are selecting a specific University/programme
  • Assist: Work one-on-one with each applicant to write, rewrite, and edit till we develop a polished submission
  • Leverage: The content can be shaped into individual submissions as per the requirement – be it an SoP, LoR, your CV, or a formal essay
